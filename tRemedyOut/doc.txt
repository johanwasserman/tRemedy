Mapping Remedy data types to Talend

Remedy:																						|	Talend:
Integer Field												2	AR_DATA_TYPE_INTEGER		|	int|Integer
Real Number Field											3	AR_DATA_TYPE_REAL			|	Double
Character Field (Entry ID, Submitter, Short Description)	4	AR_DATA_TYPE_CHAR			|	String
Diary Field													5	AR_DATA_TYPE_DIARY			|	Object
Radio Button, Drop Down List, Check Box (Status)			6	AR_DATA_TYPE_ENUM			|	String (optional: int|Integer)
Date/Time Field (Create Date)*								7	AR_DATA_TYPE_TIME			|	Date
Decimal Number Field										10	AR_DATA_TYPE_DECIMAL		|	BigDecimal
Attachment													11	AR_DATA_TYPE_ATTACH			|	Object
Currency Field												12	AR_DATA_TYPE_CURRENCY		|	String (optional: Object)
Date Field													13	AR_DATA_TYPE_DATE			|	String (optional: int|Integer)
Time Field													14	AR_DATA_TYPE_TIME_OF_DAY	|	String (optional: long|Long)

*Date/Time:
Format example: "dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm:ss a zzz" -> "25/01/2009 01:25:07 PM EST"

H			Hour in day (0~23)			22
m			Minute in hour				30
s			Second in minute			55
S			Millisecond					234

F			Day of week in month		2 (second Wed. in July)
w			Week in year				40
W			Week in month				1
a			A.M./P.M. marker			PM

'			Escape for text	Delimiter
